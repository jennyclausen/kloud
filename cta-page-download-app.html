past few years living in a city we
have observed our air quality
getting progressively worse. As
we watch this happen we continued
to confront the question of "How
do I, as a individual, make a difference?".
Over trial and error we found small
changes can make a big difference.

The Kloud has you take a short quiz so we
can better understand your air quality habits.
From there you can:
-track your statistics
-check local AQI
-keep in the loop with new trends and habits
